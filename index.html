// FAQ
> What is $HACK?
$HACK is a memecoin deployed on INK Chain — an EVM-compatible Layer 2. Total supply: 1 trillion. Zero tax. Contract renounced. LP burned.
> What is INK Chain?
INK is a fast EVM-compatible Layer 2 blockchain. To use it, add the network to MetaMask: RPC https://rpc-gel.inkonchain.com — Chain ID 57073 — Symbol ETH.
> How do I buy $HACK?
Connect your wallet on INK Chain, switch to the BUY tab, select your payment token (USDC / USDC.e / USDT0), enter amount and hit Execute Claim. 1 USD = 20,000 $HACK.
> How does the airdrop work?
Complete 4 tasks on X (follow, like+RT, share) and join Telegram. Once all tasks are done, claim 5,000 $HACK for free. One claim per wallet. On-chain verification.
> How does staking work?
Stake $HACK in the STAKE tab. Choose your lock period: 7 days (300% APY), 30 days (600% APY), or 90 days (1200% APY). Early exit returns principal only — rewards are forfeited. Small ETH fee (~$0.05) per stake.
> Is the contract safe?
Contract ownership is renounced — no one can mint new tokens or change parameters. LP is burned. All contracts are deployed on INK Chain and verifiable on the explorer.
